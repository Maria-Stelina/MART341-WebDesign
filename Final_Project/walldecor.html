Old Violin Wall Decor

Don't know what to do with an old instrument? No problem!

I was looking through the storage a while ago and came across my old violin that I used to play. It's very worn down and the strings are... hanging by a thread, so to say. I wasn't sure what to do with it, but then I remembered going to an open house in Seattle a few years ago. There was a small violin that hung up on a wall for decoration.

An idea sparked in my head. Why not just hang this violin up in my future room? But instead of just leaving it as it is, I thought it would be more fun to paint it.

Wa-la! The violin doesn't look so boring anymore.

If you have an old instrument that you don't use anymore, be bold and paint on it!
