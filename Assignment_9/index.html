The Life of Booboo



Hi, my name is Booboo


I'm a 15 year old cat that was adopted from the shelter over half a year ago. My fur parents are two sisters who work and live together at an assisted living. They give me really good petting sessions, but sometimes they show me too much affection. A cat can only take so much cuddles.

I really love treats and I beg for them often, but my owners usually refuse. I supposedly have a kidney disease and they have to restrict my diet as a result (So bothersome!). Some of the old people try to discretely sneak in some food for me (Which makes me very delighted). Anyways, I'm pretty comfortable in my new home.
