Summer Peach Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large peach, diced
  • 3-4 cups of salad greens
  • 1 cup of raspberries
  • 3 tbsps of goat cheese
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
  • 4-5 pieces of pan-seared chicken

For the dressing:

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsps dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsps lemon juice
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Coat chicken with desired amount of lemon pepper seasoning
  2. Heat 2 tbsps of olive oil over medium-high heat
  3. Once hot, pan sear the chicken slices on medium-high for 4-5 minutes on each side. Each side should be a golden brown color
  4. Set chicken aside and let cool for 10 minutes
  5. In a measuring cup, mix together together the ingredients for the dressing with a fork or whisk
  6. Place salad greens in a large bowl, and then add the raspberries, peach slices, and goat cheese on top
  7. Add chicken slices on top, and then drizzle the dressing over salad
  8. Enjoy!
